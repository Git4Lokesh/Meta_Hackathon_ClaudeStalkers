## What this run shows
1. **Format is solved** — 0.9999 format_reward means the model nails the `### TRIAGE / ### DIAGNOSIS / ### REMEDIATION` block structure on essentially every step. That part of the training problem is closed.
2. **Single-fault tasks transfer well** — task1 and example_custom both score >0.45, suggesting the policy generalizes well when the milestone phrasings are simple ("restart nginx", read a log).
3. **Specialized phrasing tasks are unreachable from cold-start** — task2/3/5/6 require utterances the base model effectively never emits at training time ("tampered", "redis is fine", "DNS misconfiguration"). With zero positive samples, GRPO has no gradient signal and the policy stays at floor reward forever.
4. **No reward hacking** — anti_hack triggers stayed at 0 across all 7,200 episodes.

## The hypothesis v6 tests
If the four flat-reward tasks really are a *cold-start* problem (no positive samples → no gradient), then warm-starting from an SFT adapter that already emits the right phrasings should unlock them. v6-SFT trains identically except it loads `GeminiHugger/war-room-sft-v1-r32` (Lokesh's SFT adapter, upcast to r=32) before the first GRPO step.
